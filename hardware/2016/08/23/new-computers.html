New computers

Aug 23, 2016

Time for new computers. I’m behind the times and reading quickly.

CPU

Broadwell-E LGA-2011, probably a Core i7 6850K at $600 for the 40 PCIe lanes.

Water cooling time? Corsair Hydro H100i GTX

Motherboard

Motherboards with X99 chipsets can have 128 GB RAM and read/write speed is around 60 GB/sec. SSDs on a M.2 interface can read/write 1500 MB/sec.
