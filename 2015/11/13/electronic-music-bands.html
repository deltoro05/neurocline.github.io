This is not an inclusive list by any means. I have favorites that I forget and then rediscover (and wonder how I forgot them), and so I expect I’ll update this list over time. It’s mostly for me to remember and to discover.

Some of my all-time favorites

  • Stockhausen
  • Walter/Wendy Carlos
  • Synergy
  • Tangering Dream
  • Vangelis
  • Jean-Michel Jarre
  • Tomita
  • Brian Eno

Some newer favorites

  • VNV Nation

And even newer (just started listening to)

  • Symbion Project
  • Kodomo

Others I want to follow up on

  • Freezepop
  • Splashdown
  • Yellow Magic Orchestra
  • Gary Numan
